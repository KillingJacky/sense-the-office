2. How to get the resource description of the offfice

The office broadcasts its resources via a MQTT topic "seeed-office-2016/well-known" at MQTT broker test.mosquitto.org. You can use any MQTT client in any language. Take mosquitto_sub as an example:

mosquitto_sub -h test.mosquitto.org -t seeed-office-2016/well-known

Then you will get messages printing like:

sub topic: seeed-office-2016/events payload: door-income

(might use websocket to expose the property of the office onto a local network in the future)

The sensor or actuator will broadcast its resource endpoints every 5 seconds.
